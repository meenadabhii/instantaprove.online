About instantaprove.online Finance

For over 25 years, we've been helping individuals and businesses achieve their financial goals with innovative solutions and personalized service.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, instantaprove.online Finance began as a small community bank with a vision to make banking more accessible and personalized. Today, we've grown into a leading financial institution serving over 2 million customers nationwide, while still maintaining our commitment to personalized service and community involvement.

Our Mission

To empower our customers to achieve their financial goals through innovative banking solutions, expert guidance, and exceptional service built on trust and transparency.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted financial partner for individuals and businesses, known for innovation, integrity, and exceptional customer experiences.

Key Milestones

1998

Founded as Community Trust Bank with first branch in San Francisco

2005

Launched online banking platform, serving customers nationwide

2012

Rebranded as instantaprove.online Finance with focus on digital innovation

2018

Reached 1 million customers and $50B in assets under management

2023

Expanded to over 185 branches with 2M+ customers nationwide

Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2023

At instantaprove.online Finance, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information automatically collected when you use our services:

Personal Information

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number, physical address)
  • Identification information (Social Security number, driver's license, passport)
  • Financial information (income, assets, account balances, transaction history)
  • Employment information (employer name, job title, income)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, features used)
  • Location information (general location based on IP address)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
  • Processing transactions and sending related information
  • Personalizing your experience and providing content recommendations
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and security risks
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Communicating with you about products, services, offers, and events

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Financial institutions and payment processors
  • Credit bureaus and identity verification services
  • Legal and regulatory authorities when required by law
  • Business partners with your consent

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information under certain circumstances
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your information
  • Data portability rights
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on consent for processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activities and to remember your preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@instantaprove.online.com
  • By phone: 1-800-instantaprove.online

Additional Disclosures

California Privacy Rights

California residents have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These include the right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose, and the right to request deletion of their personal information.

European Union Data Subjects

EU data subjects have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port their personal data.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
